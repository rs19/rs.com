Richie is an award-winning writer, filmmaker, photographer and designer. He is a graduate of Interlochen Arts Academy and a student at NYU. Richie is an entrepreneur and designer, in addition to being an award-winning filmmaker and photographer.

Richie is the Founder of Seersucker Media Group, which publishes Seersucker, a digital magazine dedicated to Millennials. SMG is deeply involved in the technology industry and constantly advocates for Millennials.

Richie's art and writing have been published in the Chicago Tribune, Huffington Post, New Jersey Star Ledger, Providence Journal, Juked, the Baltimore Review & The Interlochen Review . His award-winning short films have been accepted into over 15 film festivals across the US.

In addition to NYU and Interlochen Arts Academy, Richie has studied at General Assembly, Parsons The New School For Design and The International Center of Photography in New York City. He has interned at the Benenson Strategy Group, Code & Theory, New York City Mayor's Office of Media & Entertainment, Nola Pictures and MacGuffin Films.

Richie is currently planning a book tour for someone very cool.
