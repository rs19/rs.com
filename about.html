Richie is the founder of Seersucker Magazine, in addition to an award-winning writer, filmmaker, photographer and designer. He studies at NYU and is a graduate of Interlochen Arts Academy.

Richie planned reddit co-founder Alexis Ohanian's 200 stop, 77 college, 5 month bus tour for his book Without Their Permission , a blueprint for internet entrepreneurship.

Richie's art and writing have been published in the Chicago Tribune, New Jersey Star Ledger, Providence Journal, Juked, the Baltimore Review and The Interlochen Review . His award-winning short films have been accepted into over 15 film festivals across the US.

Richie likes graffiti and Stephen Colbert.
